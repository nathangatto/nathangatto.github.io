Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 29, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our app, which is integrated with RevenueCat for managing in-app purchases and subscriptions.

Information We Collect

We collect and process the following types of information when you use our app:

  • Purchases: We collect your purchase history to provide functionality such as subscription validation and entitlement management through RevenueCat. This data is essential for ensuring the proper functioning of in-app purchases.
  • Identifiers: If you are using the app with a custom user ID (assigned by us) or if we link purchase data to your identity (such as email or other contact information), we may use this information for user management purposes. However, we do not share your identity or use it for advertising purposes.
  • Location (locale and currency code): We may collect basic location data such as your locale and currency code for localization purposes, but we do not collect precise location data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect from you in the following ways:

  • To process and manage in-app purchases and subscriptions via RevenueCat.
  • To link purchases to your app account (if applicable) for entitlement validation and fraud prevention.

Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to handle your in-app purchase and subscription data. RevenueCat is a third-party service that helps us manage purchases and entitlements.

RevenueCat: RevenueCat collects purchase history data for the purpose of managing subscriptions and verifying in-app purchases. They do not collect sensitive personal information like health data, financial data, or browsing history. For more information, please review the RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
